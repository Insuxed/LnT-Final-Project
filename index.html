    • PuddingFest

    PuddingFest is an annual event organized by PT ChipiChapa, showcasing the latest technologies from various renowned companies in Indonesia. The event will be held online and consists of several sessions for each participating company to present their innovations. Attendees interested in witnessing these cutting-edge technologies are required to register on the PuddingFest website. Led by CEO Pak Raja, PT ChipiChapa aims to provide a platform for industry leaders to showcase their advancements and foster collaboration within the tech community.
